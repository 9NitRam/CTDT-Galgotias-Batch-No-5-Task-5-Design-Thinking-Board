## Product Chosen

**Smart Water Bottle**

## Problem Statement

People often forget to drink enough water and find it difficult to track their daily water intake. Traditional water bottles do not provide reminders or tracking features.

## Design Thinking Process

### 1. Empathize

* Conduct interviews with students and office workers.
* Observe hydration habits.
* Understand user pain points.

### 2. Define

* Users need reminders to drink water.
* Users want an easy way to track water intake.
* Users prefer affordable and smart products.

### 3. Ideate

* LED reminder lights
* Mobile app notifications
* Automatic water intake tracking
* Personalized hydration goals

### 4. Prototype

* Smart bottle with sensors
* Mobile app interface
* Bluetooth connectivity
* LED reminder system

### 5. Test

* Collect feedback from users.
* Improve features based on feedback.
* Analyze user satisfaction.

## Expected Outcome

* Improved hydration habits
* Better health awareness
* Easy water intake tracking
* Higher customer satisfaction
